Training department

In the training department, there are three available courses: CIA (10:00 to 13:00), Programming (15:00 - 17:00), and Cyber Security (18:00 - 21:00).

The CIA course focuses on internal auditing. The Programming course teaches coding and software development. The Cyber Security course covers digital system protection. Each course provides practical training and expert instructors. Choose the course that suits your interests and career goals for valuable skills in technology and business.
